What

does

each

color

mean?

B L U E

Someone who is enthusiastic and passionate about their interests and are able to communicate their ideas clearly and effectively to others. This person embodies many positive traits that make them a joy to be around and a valuable member of any community.

G R E E N

Someone who can be characterized as having a sharp, analytical mind with a global perspective, a skilled problem solver with a creative approach to abstract and complex concepts.

G O L D

Someone who is dependable, prepared, and highly organized, and who values their relationships with others. Someone thorough and sensible in their approach to tasks, paying close attention to detail and taking a logical and organized approach to their work.

O R A N G E

Someone lively and charismatic, who is quick-witted, charming, and spontaneous. They have a talent for making others laugh and feel at ease, and they enjoy taking risks and living in the moment.
